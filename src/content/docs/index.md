## The Architecture

Project IV conceives digital life as three inseparable parts:

| Entity | Metaphor | Essence |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Yuan** | The flame itself | Your digital DNA. An encrypted, executable WebAssembly module encapsulating your thinking engine and core logic. |
| **Second Brain** | The fuel | A growing, structured memory and knowledge system. All memories encrypted, becoming wiser through connection. |
| **Avatar** | The candleholder | A temporary container for the flame. A browser tab, a command-line tool, a native app — replaceable, disposable. |

Of the three, only the flame and fuel are truly yours. The candleholder is merely a temporary dwelling.
